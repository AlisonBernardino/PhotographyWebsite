Canada boasts majestic mountain ranges, including the awe-inspiring Rockies and the coastal beauty of the Coast Mountains. These landscapes offer breathtaking vistas, pristine lakes, and diverse wildlife, making them a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventurers alike.
